our mission

TF is actively involved on street children’s programs which are community-based and center-based, and in livelihood/income generating projects. Its programs evolved from a purely dole-out scheme to a more integrated, community-based development strategy to ensure impact and sustainability. Objectives of tihitna Foundation, Inc. General: To provide opportunities for the transformation of such category of children into useful and self-reliant citizens. Specific:

  • To provide street children with a safe sanctuary.
  • To re-socialize the street children such that they acquire positive spiritual, moral and personal values and attitudes in an atmosphere of love, respect, fairness, discipline and hard work.
  • To be a facilitator of education and training for the street children’s acquisition of economically productive skills.

Vision

We envision a community which is healthy, full of love, self-reliant, peaceful, with strong value system living in a productive and god-fearing society. Mission Statement:

our goal

To this end, we shall pursue a program that will provide basic needs to the abandoned, underprivileged, and unloved children and help develop their respective skills; to safeguard their respective rights; enrich their culture, value, well-being and their psychological readiness to prepare them to become good citizens of our country.
